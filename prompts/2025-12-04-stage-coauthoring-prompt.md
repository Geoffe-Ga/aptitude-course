## Project Overview

**APTITUDE** (*Adepthood: Praxis and Theory for Integrating, Transcending, and Unbridling from Determinism Effectively*) is a 10-stage, 36-week transformational course designed for "Liminal Creeps"—neurodivergent, spiritually sensitive seekers who have glimpsed awakening but struggle with embodiment, stability, and integration.

### Core Philosophy

- **Target audience**: "Drowning mystics," "Householder Shamans," digital natives who've experienced flow states and mystical insights but can't maintain basic life structures
- **Not a guru model**: Geoff explicitly rejects being a guru; participants are encouraged to customize practices
- **Integration over transcendence**: All stages are aspects of Wholeness to be integrated, not rungs to climb past
- **Cyclical, not climactic**: No permanent enlightenment; learning to surf the Archetypal Wavelength
- **Energy Scaffolding**: Adding one habit every 21 days, building sustainable momentum
- **Gift-based economy**: No one turned away for lack of funds; initiatory skin in the game

### The Archetypal Wavelength

A 6-phase sine wave pattern underlying all cycles (breath, mood, creativity, seasons, etc.):
1. **Rising** - emergence, momentum building
2. **Peaking** - culmination, resonance, flow
3. **Withdrawal** - first decline, introspection begins
4. **Diminishing** - descent continues, letting go
5. **Bottoming Out** - nadir, fallow state, surrender
6. **Restoration** - renewal, phoenix rising

Each phase has **Rx (prescribed dosage)** - healthy expression, and **OD (overdose)** - toxic expression.

**Ten Stages** organized by four "Aspect Categories":
1. **Yes-And-Ness** (Stages 1-2): Agency + Receptivity
