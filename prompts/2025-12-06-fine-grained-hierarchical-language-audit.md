#### File: `09-ultraviolet/01-what-is-ultraviolet.md`
**Line ~13:** "APTITUDE calls this **Wholeness**, and specifically the recognition of **Developmental Complexity**—not hierarchy in the sense of 'better than,' but complexity in the sense of 'requires more scaffolding.' **Severity:** MODERATE (on the right track but could be clearer)

#### File: `09-ultraviolet/02-the-mood-of-ultraviolet-wholenesshierarchy.md`

**Problem:** "some are more whole than others" and "later stages *can hold more*" directly contradicts the chords model. This is CRITICAL hierarchical language. The phrase "more whole" implies some stages are incomplete/less whole.

**Severity:** CRITICAL

Some capacities require more scaffolding—more notes learned, more practices integrated—but this doesn't make them more whole or valuable. A single note played with presence can be as whole as a complex jazz chord. The question is: which does this moment call for?"

## Cross-Cutting Patterns

### Pattern 1: "Previous/Earlier/Later Stage"
**Files affected:** Multiple across Yellow, Ultraviolet, Clear Light

**Problem:** Language of "previous stage" or "earlier stage" reinforces temporal/linear sequence even when used descriptively.

**Severity:** MODERATE (systemic pattern)

**Suggested fix:** Use "different stages" or refer to stages by name/color instead of temporal position.

**Examples:**
- "each previous stage" → "each stage you've learned"
- "earlier stages" → "other stages" or "foundational stages"
- "later stages" → "more complex stages" or "integrative stages"

---

### Pattern 2: Spatial Metaphors (Ascending/Descending/Heights/Depths)
**Files affected:** Beige 14, Green 22, Ultraviolet 05, Ultraviolet 12

**Problem:** Spatial metaphors (ascending, heights, depths, rising above) subtly reinforce hierarchical thinking even when used poetically.

**Severity:** MODERATE

**Recommendation:**
- When referring to **energy/wavelength phases**: KEEP spatial language (rising/falling energy is appropriate)
- When referring to **stages/development**: AVOID spatial language
- When referring to **chakras**: KEEP (higher/lower chakras is anatomically accurate)

---

### Pattern 3: "Can't...Until" Constructions
**Files affected:** Beige 02, Green 22, Ultraviolet 01, Ultraviolet 10

**Problem:** "You can't do X until Y" can imply prerequisites/sequence, BUT most uses are accurate (you genuinely can't play a complex chord until you learn the individual notes). This is about pedagogical sequence, not stage hierarchy.

**Severity:** MINOR (mostly appropriate)

**Recommendation:** Keep most of these, but ensure they're clearly about LEARNING sequence, not about one stage being better than another.
