# Hierarchical Language Map (Issue #5)

The model passage (from *Free Will at Beige*) signified a unidirectional march
through the stages: being "at" a stage as a location, making it "to" adulthood
"still at" Beige, stages as territory you pass through and leave. The repo-wide
sweep below used that paragraph as the template.

**The editorial rule applied** (from the issue): stages are *unlocked in
order*, roughly as Wilber theorizes — decentration, making the current
subjective experience an object that can be examined through the lens of the
next stage. But once unlocked, a stage's behaviors and concerns are never "in
the past." They arise when needed, skillfully or discordantly depending on
dosage.

## Reviewed and deliberately kept

- **"Previous Stage" navigation links** (all stage READMEs) — course sequence,
  not developmental rank.
- **"more advanced practice/methods"** (`04-blue/07`, `09-ultraviolet/21`,
  `10-clearlight/12`) — refers to practice difficulty/duration, not person rank.
- **`09-ultraviolet/11` ("someone stuck at Orange… they're so unconscious")** —
  the Spiritual Superiority *shadow* chapter quoting the pathological mindset
  in order to dismantle it. The hierarchy is the bug being displayed.
- **`09-ultraviolet/15`, `07-yellow/11` ("more evolved than them")** — same:
  the ego-trap voice, explicitly framed as a trap.
- **`10-clearlight/15`** ("you don't graduate past the earlier stages — you
  integrate them all") — already the chords thesis.
- **`09-ultraviolet/01`, `09-ultraviolet/10`** — explicitly argue stages are
  not "less evolved"; on-message.
- **"Earlier/previous stages" as course chronology** (`05-orange/01`,
  `05-orange/04`, `09-ultraviolet/16`, `09-ultraviolet/20`,
  `resources/about.md`) — refers to the 9-month sequence in which capacities
  are unlocked, which the issue affirms is real.
- **"a culture stuck in Beige"** (`02-purple/01`) — stuck *playing one note*,
  which is exactly the chords-model critique.
- **`resources/aptitude-stages.md` "a serving of each rung"** — the food-
  pyramid metaphor requires every rung daily; anti-ladder in substance.

## Patterns to watch in future writing

Being "at" a stage as a location · "still at X" · "make it to" a stage ·
"advance/progress/climb through" · "drop back to earlier stages" ·
"more/less developed" people · stages as past tense ("your past X behaviors").
Prefer: unlocked/not yet unlocked · loudest note · leading with a chord ·
Rx/OD dosage of a note that is always available.
