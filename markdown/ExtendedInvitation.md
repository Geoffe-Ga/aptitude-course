Wanderers!

Welcome! You’ve taken a step toward Adepthood. A stage
of development past adulthood where you can
actually trust in your capacity for Free Will.
Where you choose your own path. APTITUDE is a
ten-stage, 36-week rite of
passage, an adventure in self-integration,
shadow reclamation, blissful union, and radical agency.

It’s a silly welcome: one to the path you were already
walking. Based on nothing but you clicking the link that led you here,
I’m betting you’ve got the karma to
belong—the childhood
trauma and neurodivergence, perhaps, but also a familiarity with the
flow states of shamanic epiphany and probably a brief glimpse or two of
the face of the divine.

Maybe most of all, you have a gnawing sense that
humans are simply biological machines; slaves to social roles; victims
of inevitable, self-sabotaging habits and addictions.

You might go so far as to express it using the
singularly disempowering slogan, “Free Will is
an illusion.”

You’ve felt it. You’ve raved—both a dancing partier and
a ranting madman—from your perch on the edges. You’ve dawdled your toes
into transcendence. Felt the paradox of being both more than and less
than human. You are a [Liminal Trickster Mystic](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://creekmasons.com/p/liminal-trickster-mystics&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1764912818367395&usg=AOvVaw1Kacva30QZabg5eoRWqr16), or
at least, you suspect you might be.

And you’ve been called.

APTITUDE—Adepthood: Praxis and
Theory for Integrating, Transcending, and Unbridling from Determinism
Effectively—is neither a dogma nor a rigid
hierarchy that only by summiting can one be enlightened, whole or Good
Enough. Although the course comprises both theory and the way to put
theory into action—praxis—the ingredients of the experience of Adepthood
are already available to you right
now.

It just helps to learn the scales before you forget
everything, shut off the analyzing mind, and let go. Even though you
don’t necessarily use it when the moment comes, being able to read sheet
music and understand music theory helps you
flow, expressing your
unique aliveness like an improvised jazz solo.

But let’s be clear—this is
not about
transcendence in the way a Creep’s spiritual bypassing ego might prefer.

No.

This is about the full, messy, embodied experience of
being alive, about pulling every ounce of juice from your human
incarnation. It’s about awakening, sure, but it’s also about
getting your shit
together.

at all the kind of path where you’ll turn to a pillar of salt if you
look back. Although some of the most cutting edge approaches to

spiral, as they “transcend” them, those of us belonging to the
subculture that obsesses with self-actualization sometimes seem to look
down on the “woke” Greens or the “sheeple” Blues or anyone else
“beneath” them on the hierarchy.

Wholeness isn’t about outgrowing or leaving aspects of
the human experience behind. It’s about
expressing them in the maximally effective,
healthy, authentic way available.

Sign up!

Get yourself a guide for moving from
Liminal Creep to Whole
Adept.

let it be known: I am no

whimsically weave through game trails. We make our own adventure.

Your playful curiosity, authentic self-expression,

Throughout the program, I’ll be presenting a
framework for
Wholeness into which you

### A Final Note on the Curriculum of the APTITUDE Course

Even with the slow and careful pace of APTITUDE, we
might not expect the full fruition of any Stage, but particularly the
final three, in just nine months.

However!

According to Pragmatic Dharma luminaries like
[Daniel Ingram](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://www.mctb.org/&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1764912818376575&usg=AOvVaw2AoWIifknpzIfmoJuie_bP) and
[Culadasa John Yates](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://culadasa.com&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1764912818376723&usg=AOvVaw1hwnT_HWwSjBJSh62jjj8q), months or
years, rather than the mythical “decades or lifetimes,” is actually a
totally reasonable timeline for Awakening. And if their practice
prescriptions—incorporated into Blissy Meditation—want for anything,
it’s exactly what is provided via Energy Scaffolding and in the Stages
prior to Yellow: prep work.

That’s fine, we needed their clear, unambiguous
meditation instruction. I haven’t found better or more practical advice
anywhere in the 60+ books and countless podcasts I’ve consumed in the
last two years of dedicated research that went into this Course. Much
less in the decade plus of more casual practice prior.

But if you’re reading this, I’m betting what you need
now is not meditation as a form of athleticism—what you need is to
clear away the
obstacles to consistent
practice so that all the techniques you’ve
learned about can finally be put into action.

In general, we in the West are starving for a
framework for the ethical prerequisites that make diligent practice
possible. We need a preamble to concentration and mindfulness that looks
a bit like intentional detoxification from the poisonous attitudes,
habits, mood states, and addictions written into each of us by Default
Reality.

One that encourages the embrace of Wholeness: of
the full spectrum of
human potentiality.

Not
just Nonduality or
Emptiness. All Ten
Stages, fully integrated. Fully Whole and
holy.

Sound desirable?

planet.
