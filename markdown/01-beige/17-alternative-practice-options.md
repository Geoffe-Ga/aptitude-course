## Alternative Practice Options

Although the 5-4-3-2-1 technique is like a great bit of Pratyahara (or yogic “turning inward”), many people find it hard to remember the order or keep the numbers straight.

That extra bit of thinking is part of what grounds you, and employing patience toward it will definitely lower your activation level before you shoot into space, but as with all the Stages, I’ll offer some alternative Practices so you can choose what suits you best.

- Square - Circle - Triangle
  x5: Find things that are those shapes in the room you’re in. Go in a cycle 5 times.
- Colors of the Rainbow x 3:
  Silently name things in the room you’re in that are the colors of the rainbow. Start with red and go in order three times.
- Clench/Relax body scan: Begin
  with your toes, clench on inhale, relax on exhale. Work your way up your body (and back down if you like). Works especially well in savasana.
- Stand Barefoot Outdoors: Take
  two to three minutes to touch some grass! Stand still with your arms at your sides. Focus on the feeling of your weight being securely held by the Earth as it presses up into the souls and balls of your feet.
  Remember that no matter where you are, the Earth’s core is always directly beneath you. Tap into it.
