## Energy Scaffolding

If you aren’t called to stick with the default Habits, your APTITUDE journey hinges on the process of Energy Scaffolding: the sacred art of building your life one Habit at a time. Every 21 days, you’ll add a new behavior to your stack—a Habit that gives more energy back to you than it takes to engage with it. You choose habits that feed you.

It is imperative that you focus completely on a single Habit and build up slow but steady progress. You may feel painfully impatient with only adding or subtracting a single new behavior every three weeks, but after you write down everything you want your perfect life to have, multiply it by three weeks and divide by 52 to get the number of years it will take… I’m betting you’ll be surprised by how soon you can have your perfect life.

And it will definitely be sooner than you’d get that life if you were stuck stutter-starting as I’m sure you have been for the last long while. As Tim Ferris says, many of us are like the donkey stuck between a bale of hay and a trough of water, both hungry and thirsty but unable to decide which need to sate first. A donkey that doesn’t realize it can do one for a bit and then come back for the other, partially recharged, might die of both starvation and dehydration, while indecisive in the middle.

Still we have to prioritize. If you’re like me you’ve probably started and abandoned a million plans for self-improvement.This might not even be the first time that you’ve adopted a plan to intentionally tweak your daily Habit stack. But Energy Scaffolding is different.

You’ll use the energy from the last habit to fuel the next. Slowly. Patiently. Like building a bonfire one twig at a time.

This is how we reclaim our doing. This is how we begin to trust ourselves. This is how we prove to our nervous systems that we are no longer at the mercy of our moods or cravings. We are choosing, deliberately, to show up.
