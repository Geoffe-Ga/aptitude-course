## The Long Game: What Happens After a Year

After 365 days of 45-minute sits, you'll have logged ~275 hours of meditation. That's not mastery—Culadasa estimates 10,000 hours for full awakening—but it's a foundation.

Here's what typically shifts:

- **Attention becomes trainable.** You can focus when you need to and release when you don't.

- **Emotions become workable.** You still feel everything, but you're not *controlled* by it. There's space.

- **Patterns become visible.** You see your conditioning in real-time. And seeing it gives you choice.

- **Presence becomes accessible.** You can drop into the present moment, even in chaos. It's a skill now, not an accident.

- **Compassion deepens.** For yourself and others. You've seen how much of behavior is driven by unconscious forces. Judgment softens.

This is the gift of the daily practice. Not transcendence. Not perfection. Just a gradual, undeniable increase in capacity. In freedom. In wholeness.

That's Yellow. That's the path.
