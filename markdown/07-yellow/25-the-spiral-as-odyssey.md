## The Spiral as Odyssey

APTITUDE is your Odyssey. Each stage is an island. Each practice is a test. And Yellow is the return—the integration of everything you've learned on the journey.

You've grounded in Beige (built the foundation).

You've received in Purple (learned to listen to the symbolic).

You've asserted in Red (claimed your power).

You've connected in Blue (found your people).

You've achieved in Orange (built competence).

You've descended in Green (met your shadow).

And now, at Yellow, you're being asked to *weave it all together*. To become Odysseus stringing the bow. To act from wholeness instead of fragmentation.

That's the work. That's the practice. That's Yellow.
