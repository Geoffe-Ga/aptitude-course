## The Rhythm of Yang and Yin

Orange taught you Yang: effort, intensity, building capacity. Green teaches you Yin: softening, receiving, allowing recovery.

But here's the key: they're not opposites. They're *partners*.

You can't stay in Yang forever. You'll burn out. You can't stay in Yin forever. You'll stagnate.

The rhythm is the thing. Push, then rest. Activate, then integrate. Ascend, then descend.

This is the wisdom of the seasons. Summer (Yang) and Winter (Yin). The agricultural cycle. The lunar cycle. The breath itself—inhale and exhale.

Green is where you learn to honor the exhale. To stop treating rest as failure. To recognize that the deepest growth happens in the pauses, not in the striving.

Active Recovery is the practice of the pause.
