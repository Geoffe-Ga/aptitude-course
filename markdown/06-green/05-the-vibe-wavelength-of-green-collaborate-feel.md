## The Mode of the Wavelength of Green: Collaborate (Feel)

Green continues the Collaborate Mode, but shifts from **Do** (Orange) to **Feel**. Where Orange collaborated through strategic action, Green collaborates through *emotional attunement*. You're learning to work with others not by dividing tasks, but by sensing the field. By feeling into what's needed. By letting the relational space inform the work.

This is the Wavelength of the support group, the circle, the heart-centered community. It's where healing happens not through doing but through *being together*. Through witnessing and being witnessed. Through holding and being held.

The six phases of Green's Wavelength map the territory of authentic connection—and the ways it can go sideways:

**Rising: Rx = Connection | OD = Oversharing**

**Peaking: Rx = Belonging | OD = Megalomania**

**Withdrawal: Rx = Retirement | OD = Social Anxiety**

**Diminishing: Rx = Unwinding | OD = Alienation**

**Bottoming Out: Rx = Repose | OD = Isolation**

**Restoration: Rx = Vulnerability | OD = Bitterness**

This is the rhythm of relational work. Of showing up, connecting deeply, retreating to integrate, and returning renewed. It's the cycle of intimacy. And like all cycles, it has medicine and poison at every phase.

We'll explore each in depth. But first, notice this: Green's Wavelength is *slower* than Orange's. Where Orange moved quickly (hypothesize, experiment, data, analysis, synthesis, question), Green lingers. It takes its time. It honors the pauses.

Because feelings can't be rushed. The body has its own timeline. And Green is where you learn to trust that.
