## The Long Game: What Happens After a Year at Teal

After 365 days of Dog Walkin' Shamanism (60 min, 4x/week) and Baby Waterfall Conventions (daily), here's what typically shifts:

- **The Witness becomes accessible.** You can drop into witnessing consciousness in the middle of chaos. It's no longer a rare state—it's a capacity you can activate.

- **Reactivity decreases dramatically.** You still have emotional responses, but there's space around them. You catch yourself before you say the thing you'll regret. Before you send the angry email. Before you collapse into the old pattern.

- **Presence becomes baseline.** You're not constantly in your head anymore. You're here. Now. With what is. And that simple shift changes everything.

- **Equanimity deepens.** The highs don't send you to the moon. The lows don't destroy you. You're learning to surf the wavelength with grace.

- **Spontaneity returns.** Paradoxically, the more you witness, the more spontaneous you become. You're not planning every move. You're responding to what's alive in the moment. You're trusting the process.

This is the fruit of Teal. Not transcendence. Not escape. Just a deepening capacity to be fully human—awake, responsive, and free.
