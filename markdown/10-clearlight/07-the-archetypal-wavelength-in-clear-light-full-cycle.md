## The Archetypal Wavelength in Clear Light: Full Cycle

At Clear Light, you don't transcend the Wavelength. You *master* it. You see the full cycle—Rising, Peaking, Withdrawal, Diminishing, Bottoming Out, Restoration—and you know how to work with each phase skillfully.

The image descriptions from `ImageDescriptions.md` show this beautifully. There are infographics for Clear Light that map:

- **Influence during Highs and Lows**: How external forces impact you at Peaking and Bottoming Out. At Peaking, you're vulnerable to inflation, grandiosity, believing your own hype. At Bottoming Out, you're vulnerable to despair, collapse, the belief that it will never get better. Clear Light teaches you to see these influences *and* not be moved by them.

- **Influence during Rising and Restoration**: How support and encouragement can lift you when energy is returning. The people, practices, and conditions that help you rise skillfully—without grasping, without forcing.

- **The Practice Itself**: The final infographic shows the practice cycle moving through the Wavelength with the teaching **"Obstacles ARE the Path."** This is the culmination. Every phase—even the difficult ones—is sacred. Every obstacle is an opportunity. Every descent is a teacher.

At Clear Light, you've internalized Chögyam Trungpa's teachings:
- **"When things are going well, don't relax."** (Rising/Peaking) Stay present. Don't get seduced by success.
- **"When things are going poorly, don't panic."** (Diminishing/Bottoming Out) Stay grounded. Don't collapse into despair.

These aren't just platitudes. They're *practices*. And at Clear Light, they're your baseline.

Let's walk through each phase one more time—not as Rx/OD (you've mastered that), but as *qualities of presence*:

**1. RISING**: You feel energy returning. Motivation building. The medicine is to *ride it* without grasping. Let the momentum carry you, but stay rooted. Don't believe the hype.

**2. PEAKING**: You're at your best. Everything's flowing. The medicine is *gratitude without attachment*. Enjoy it fully. And let it pass when it's time.

**3. WITHDRAWAL**: The energy ebbs. The initial clarity fades. The medicine is *trust*. The descent is not failure. It's the natural rhythm. Let go without collapsing.

**4. DIMINISHING**: You're in the trough. Nothing feels good. The medicine is *patience*. This too shall pass. You've been here before. You know the way through.

**5. BOTTOMING OUT**: You've hit the bottom. The medicine is *rest*. Stop trying to fix it. Just be here. Let the ground hold you.

**6. RESTORATION**: Energy is returning. The medicine is *recommitment*. Not to something new, but to the same practice, the same path, the same truth. Again and again and again.

This is the Wavelength at Clear Light. Not transcended. *Embodied*. You don't fight the cycle. You *are* the cycle. And you're also the awareness that watches it unfold.
