### <span class="c6 c7">From Liminal Creep to Whole Adept: Transformation Made Possible by APTITUDE </span>

<span class="c2">You know the </span><span class="c9 c2"><a
href="https://www.google.com/url?q=https://creekmasons.com/p/im-a-creep&amp;sa=D&amp;source=editors&amp;ust=1764912924390900&amp;usg=AOvVaw0O4ds30GBvo0qRtgbiv-Et"
class="c4">Creep archetype</a></span><span class="c2">. A touch of
loving self-deprecation inspired by </span><span class="c2 c9"><a
href="https://www.google.com/url?q=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v%3DXFkzRNyygfk&amp;sa=D&amp;source=editors&amp;ust=1764912924391036&amp;usg=AOvVaw0mpteUohUlokmzvjtmrDVQ"

</span><span class="c6">been</span><span class="c5 c2"> the Creep.
</span>

<span class="c2 c5">The one whose insight is sharp enough to cut
themself on it, but whose life is a dull mess. The one who’s glimpsed
enlightenment at 3AM in a haze of nicotine and caffeine but who can’t
seem to hold a job. The one who either rails against the system or
dissolves, unable to convince themself that matter is what
matters.</span>

<span class="c5 c2">The Liminal Creep knows they have a Buddha nature,
but sometimes forgets their social security number.</span>

<span class="c2">The Whole Adept, on the other hand, has integrated.
They are neither a guru nor a dropout, neither a zealot nor a nihilist.
They are awake
</span><span class="c6">and</span><span class="c2"> effective. Mystical
</span><span class="c6">and</span><span class="c2"> grounded. They are
in radical self-ownership, </span><span class="c6">capable of playing
the game without the game playing them.</span>

<span class="c2">APTITUDE is the bridge between Creep and Adept. It
takes you from self-sabotage to self-mastery. From disempowered
rebellion to </span><span class="c6">power-with</span><span class="c2">,
from alienation to genuine belonging. It teaches you
</span><span class="c0">how to swim in the mystical without drowning in
psychosis.</span>

<span class="c2">More than anything else, to me, Liminal Creeps
represent an untapped resource of—typically
neurodivergent—</span><span class="c6">seekers who are ready to be
finders</span><span class="c2">. Cast-aside shamans who I hope can

of service to the
</span><span class="c6">polycrisis</span><span class="c5 c2">—the

</span>
