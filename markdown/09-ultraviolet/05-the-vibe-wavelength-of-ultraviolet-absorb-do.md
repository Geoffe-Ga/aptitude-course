## The Mode of the Wavelength of Ultraviolet: Absorb (Do/Feel)

Ultraviolet introduces the fifth Mode: **Absorb**. Where Inhabit was about building yourself, Express was about externalizing, Collaborate was about co-creating, and Integrate was about synthesizing, Absorb is about *taking in the whole*.

You're no longer just participating in reality. You're *receiving* it. Drinking it in. Letting it penetrate you so fully that the boundary between inner and outer dissolves.

But Ultraviolet is **Absorb (Do/Feel)**—the Mode where the two currents finally merge. Doing and Feeling stop being separate channels here: you *intentionally open* (the Do) in order to be filled (the Feel). You're creating the conditions for absorption through rigorous practice.

This is the Wavelength of the jhana practitioner. The meditator cultivating states so refined that the mind becomes like still water—capable of reflecting reality without distortion. You're absorbing the teachings. Absorbing the transmission. Absorbing the truth of impermanence, suffering, and non-self until they're no longer concepts—they're *seen*.

The six phases of Ultraviolet's Wavelength map the territory of deepening realization:

**Rising: Rx = Ascending | OD = Transcending**

**Peaking: Rx = Clarity | OD = Superiority**

**Withdrawal: Rx = Discernment | OD = Judgment**

**Diminishing: Rx = Humility | OD = Humiliation**

**Bottoming Out: Rx = Devotion | OD = Dogma**

**Restoration: Rx = Recommitment | OD = Righteousness**

This is the rhythm of the serious practitioner. The one who sits retreats. Who deepens year after year. Who is committed to the path not as a hobby, but as *the work*.

We'll explore each phase. But first, recognize: Ultraviolet's Wavelength is about *going deeper*. Not wider. Deeper. Into the same practices. The same insights. Until they stabilize as your baseline reality.
